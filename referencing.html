Referencing

Writing assignments at university will be in form of essay, reports or papers and dissertation in the last years of an undergraduate programme. There is a whole page on mynapier to help you with writing but the most useful tools for us were academic phrasebank and Mendeley. Mendeley is a software which allows you to organise, collaborate and discover, as well as use the citation plugin to cite as you write in Microsoft Word or LibreOffice.
