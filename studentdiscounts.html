Student discounts

NUS card

As a student you can buy NUS card to get lots of discounts such as contact lenses, eating out, entertainment, cinema, Spotify, fashion, finance, gym, music, supermarkets, Railcard, broadband or uni essentials just to mention some.

To buy a card just enter your email address at NUS website and in the place of study add Edinburgh Napier University Students' Association for Napier student or Edinburgh College Students' Association for Edinburgh college students. You can buy it for a year for £12, 2 years for £22 or 3 years for £32. If you send it to your uni or college association, the postage is free.

Ridacard

Get Ridacard to use Lothian buses. You can pay weekly (£15), four weekly (£45) or annually (£525). To buy it, pop into one of the travelshops. They will take your picture so dress nice ;) You can then set up direct debit or top it up at many of PayPoint agents, for example Co-op.

Other

I also recommend to sign up for Unidays.

Another great website worth to register on isStudent beans with discount in KFC, Uber, Netflix and many more.

Don’t forget to check your student discounts every time you pay, many places offer students discount.
