Funding

Fees

Most Scottish and European Union fulltime students will not have to pay for tuition fees. No application is required to apply for fees for full-time further education courses as your eligibility for fees will be considered automatically at enrolment.

College Bursaries

Students on full-time Further Education courses can apply to the college for a bursary. A bursary award is typically made up of three elements; maintenance allowance, travel and course kit costs. Bursary awards are means-tested against parents/partners income and you may not be eligible for all elements of a bursary award.

Previous Study If you have previously received financial help for a full time course(s) and you wish to study at the same level, or a lower level course your eligibility for funding may be affected. If you have previously studied at a higher level e.g. HNC/HND or degree level, your eligibility for funding may also be affected.

If you decide to study in Edinburgh, you have a big chance to be eligible for funding For more information and to register for funding such as student loan go to and click on login in top right corner where you can register your details. You must apply for SAAS every year you are studying if you want help with paying your fees.
