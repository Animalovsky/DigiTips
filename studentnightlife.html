Student nightlife

For cheap start of the night hit Bar Salsa on cowgate. It’s very cheap and busy. You can then follow to many of the clubs on cowgate – The Bongoclub, Sneaky Pete’s, The cabaret Voltaire, The Liquid rooms, The La Belle Angele and many more. I recommend sneakers to those clubs.

If you want to dress up hit the George street, El Barrio, Lulu, Shanghai just to mention a few.

Favorite student pubs are Malones and Three sisters.

And don’t forget when Edinburgh is sunny everyone goes to sit in the Meadows!
