Life at Napier

The academic year at Edinburgh Napier University runs from late September until mid September the following year. It is split into 3 15 week teaching blocks – trimesters. Most students attend only first 2 trimesters.

ENSA

The academic year at Edinburgh Napier University runs from late September until mid September the following year. It is split into 3 15 week teaching blocks – trimesters. Most students attend only first 2 trimesters (third one is the one used for when you fail a module or you attend it in nursing a midwifery course).
