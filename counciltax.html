Council Tax

As a full-time student you probably wont have to pay your Council Tax bill. To qualify as a student, you must either study for 24 weeks for 21 hours per week in each academic year be under 20 years old, have left home and be studying for a qualifying course for at least 12 hours a week for three months - qualifying courses include A levels, Highers, HNDs, HNCs and Level 4 SVQs be a foreign language assistant working in a school or other educational establishment and be registered with the British Council.

You can get 100% exemption if you live in a flat with only students or 25% if there is at least one student so I suggest to look for flat with your student mates.

You can apply online but I suggest to find a local council office and get it sorted there, council is very busy and it takes much longer if done online

You are liable to pay your council tax if youre changing from one institution to another, so for example our exemption was 100% at 2 years at college, but I had to pay break between college and uni (june to September).
